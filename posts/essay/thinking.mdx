I'm sure everyone's heard of the advice "think for yourself."
It's advice that, in some ways, all of us could stand to follow more often.
A lot of opinions we hold and choices we make are borrowed,
and it is important to be aware of that.
But if you want to get anything done, it's impossible
to question and scrutinize everything you come across.
So where do we draw the line?
